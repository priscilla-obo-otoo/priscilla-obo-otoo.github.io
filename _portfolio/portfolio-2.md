## Overview

Statistical consulting project providing data review and 
analytical guidance for a cross-sectional study on neonatal 
jaundice and G6PD deficiency among 200 neonates in Ghana.

## Contributions

- Built a data cleaning and validation pipeline in R
- Conducted descriptive and inferential statistical analysis
- Produced a results report structured for manuscript submission

## Tools

R · tidyverse · ggplot2

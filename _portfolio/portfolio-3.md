## Overview

Co-presenter of the Pathway Analysis series on 
[Stat-Inf](https://infstat.github.io/), a community 
dedicated to statistics and computer science.

The series covers key pathway analysis methods, ORA, GSEA, 
and enrichment visualization, through accessible video content.
